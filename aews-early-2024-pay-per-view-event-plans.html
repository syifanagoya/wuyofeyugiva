AEWs Early 2024 Pay-Per-View Event Plans

July 15, 2024
blog

AEW has been making waves in the wrestling world with its expanding schedule of pay-per-view events. The latest event on the horizon is AEW World End, set to take place at the Nassau Veterans Memorial Coliseum in Long Island, New York, with the headlining match featuring AEW World Heavyweight Champion MJF facing off against Samoa Joe.

However, fans have been speculating about whether AEW would host another pay-per-view event in January or February, given their increased PPV schedule in recent months. In the latest Wrestling Observer Newsletter, Dave Meltzer clarified that AEW does not plan to hold another PPV event before Revolution.

As of now, here is the list of AEW events that have been announced:

  • AEW Worlds End PPV – Saturday, December 30, 2023, at the Nassau Veterans Memorial Coliseum in Long Island, New York.
  • AEW Dynamite/Rampage: Various dates and locations in January and February.
  • AEW Revolution – Sunday, March 3, 2023, at the Greensboro Coliseum in Greensboro, NC, featuring Sting’s retirement match.

While there is no PPV event scheduled for January or February, the AEW schedule is packed with television events, including AEW Dynamite and AEW Collision, providing fans with plenty of wrestling action to enjoy.

It remains to be seen if AEW will hold a PPV event in April, as they traditionally host Double or Nothing in May. AEW continues to expand its reach and deliver exciting wrestling content to its devoted fanbase.
