Who Is Sid Owen?

Sid Owen is a British actor and TV personality famous for appearing as Ricky Butcher in the BBC One soap opera EastEnders.

Born on January 12, 1972, in England, Owen has appeared on many shows including Strictly Come Dancing and How Clean is Your House.

He began starting on EastEnders in 1988 alongside Danniella Westbrook and Patsy Palmer.

Owen left EastEnders in 2000 to try his hands on music, and later made a comeback in a spin-off episode in 2002.

On TV, Owen appeared on an episode of Blankety Blank, the prison drama Bad Girls, The Jump, The Bill and in the season 5 of I’m a Celebrity, Get Me Out of Here!, where he finished third place.

He also played the role of Prince Charming in Snow White and the Seven Dwarfs and served as a TV co-host on To Buy or Not to Buy with Melinda Messenger.
