Early Life and Career Beginnings

Born on November 8, 1972, in Deep River, Connecticut, Mol showed an early interest in performing arts.

Mol’s career breakthrough came with her role in The Notorious Bettie Page. She continued to impress audiences with her performances in 3:10 to Yuma and Manchester by the Sea.

Collaborating with renowned directors and actors, Mol’s filmography boasts more than 30 feature films, each showcasing her versatility and talent.

Television Success

Gretchen Mol is best known for her role as Gillian Darmody in HBO’s Boardwalk Empire. This series not only elevated her status among leading wealthy TV actresses but also earned her critical acclaim.

Theater Contributions
