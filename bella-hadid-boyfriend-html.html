Who Is Bella Hadids Rumored Boyfriend? Everything About the Models Relationship

Bella Hadid has made her love Instagram public by posting a PDA-filled photo with her alleged boyfriend, Marc Kalman.

On July 8, 2021, the American model, who has close to 44 million followers on Instagram, grammed a picture of her kissing Kalman on the last slide of her France photo dump.

Hadid, who is traveling between Cannes and Paris to attend the Cannes Film Festival and Paris Fashion Week, stated in the caption that she was having the time of her life and alluded to being loved.

Her fans quickly took notice of the romantic moment and dropped their reactions on the comment section.

"LAST PIC OMG IM SCREAMING," one wrote.

Another added, "You slipped in that 8th pic like we wouldn't notice."

In the midst of congratulatory messages, fans are also curious to learn about her alleged boyfriend since Hadid has not opened about him so far.

Hadid's Rumoured Boyfriend Is an Art Director

Kalman is an art director who has been working closely with Kylie Jenner's boyfriend, Travis Scott, for a long time.

According to his website, he has also worked in the fashion industry by designing logo art for Made Fashion Week and fashion brand A Bathing Ape.

In addition, he was associated with a Smoke x Mirrors eyewear campaign and served as a fashion assistant for the New York Times and T Magazine.

The 1985 magazine’s writer Periel Aschenbrand, who interviewed Kalman and his work partner Corey Damon Black in December 2014, described the art director as a good listener, a “young Fabien Baron, Charles Saatchi and Steve Jobs all rolled into one,” and “cautious and calculating.”

Kalman does not have public social media accounts, keeping his personal life very private. However, he has a private Instagram handle that has a little over 3 thousand followers.

Their Relationship Timeline

Hollywood’s newest pair, Hadid and Kalman, were first romantically linked in early June after they were spotted out on a lunch date together. They reportedly met through mutual friends.

All this while, both of them have kept mum around their alleged relationship.

But the couple started trending on the media when Hadid posted two back-to-back pictures with him on June 8, 2021 — one on her Instagram post and another on Instagram stories.

Hadid and Kalman are yet to confirm the relationship.

Hadid's Dating History Over the Years

Hadid, the younger sister of Gigi Hadid, has been romantically linked to a handful of Hollywood A-listers through the years.

Her most recently known relationship was with the Canadian singer, The Weeknd. The two had an on-again-off relationship between May 2015 and August 2019.

Between their relationship, both dated other partners, too. For instance, The Weeknd took Selena Gomez to the May 2017 Met Gala.

As for Hadid, she reportedly dated the Cleveland Browns wide receiver Odell Beckham Jr. circa the mid-2010s.

She was also rumored with rapper Drake, but none of them ever commented on the rumors.

Hadid, who has a very close friendship with Fai Khadra, is often linked with him and tagged as a couple.

In October 2020, her dating rumors with Jack Nicholson‘s grandson Duke Nicholson made the headlines, too. But Hadid’s spokesperson denied the romance.
