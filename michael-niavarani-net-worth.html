Who is Michael Niavarani?

When was Michael Niavarani born?

He was born on April 29, 1968, in Vienna, Austria.

What are some of Michael Niavarani’s notable achievements?

Niavarani has won the Austrian Kabarett Award in 2010 and is a three-time winner of the Romy award.

Has he written any books?
