What is Klaus Meine's Net Worth?

Klaus Meine is a German vocalist who has a net worth of $75 million. Klaus Meine was born in Hannover, Germany in May 1948. He is best known for being the lead singer of the rock band Scorpions. Scorpions formed in 1965 and Meine was actually the band's fifth lead singer. Klaus and band mate Rudolph Schenker are the only band members who have appeared on every single Scorpions album.

They released their debut studio album Lonesome Crow in 1972 and have released a total of 18 studio albums including Blackout in 1982 which reached #1 in the Netherlands, Savage Amusement in 1988 which reached #1 in Finland, and Crazy World in 1990 which reached #1 in both Germany and Austria. Their biggest hit is the single "Wind of Change" which reached #1 in six countries. Scorpions also had success with the singles "Still Loving You" and "Send Me An Angel". Meine was named the #22 Top Heavy Metal Vocalist of All Time by Hit Parader in 2006.
