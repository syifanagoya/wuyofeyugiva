Career

At the beginning of his career, Kevin Owens used to wrestle in Jacques Rougeau’s promotion. In 2003, he joined the IWS (International Wrestling Syndicate). In 2004, he defeated PCO and became the IWS World Heavyweight Champion. He remained in IWS till 2009.

He has also wrestled in the Combat Zone Wrestling from 2004 to 2006. In 2005, Owens became the CZW Iron Man Champion by pinning Franky The Mobster. He successfully defended his title against several big names.

From 2005 to 2008, Kevin Owens was a part of the Pro Wrestling Guerrilla. In there, he defeated A.J. Styles to win the PWG Championship on August 2005. From 2007 to 2010, he wrestled in the Ring of Honor. He won the ROH Tag Team Championship and was in Wrestling’s Worst Nightmare from 2011 to 2013.

Later, Owens returned to PWG and got signed with the WWE in 2014. He was seen in WWE’s NXT. In 2015, he became the NXT Champion. In September 2015, Owens became the WWE Intercontinental Champion.

He was drafted to RAW in 2016, and later he won the WWE Universal Championship against three other wrestlers. He was the co-captain of Team Raw along with Chris Jericho. In 2017, he feuded with Shane McMahon following which, he was fired from the WWE along with Sami Zayn.

Awards & Achievements

Kevin Owens has won the WWE Universal Championship, Intercontinental Championship, and the United States Championship. He was named as the Best Brawler from Wrestling Observer Newsletter from 2010 to 2012. He is also a one-time Ring of Honor World Champion. In 2017, he featured in the list of top 500 singles wrestlers in the PWI 500. He has won championships in All American Wrestling, Combat Zone Wrestling, International Wrestling Syndicate, and Pro Wrestling Guerrilla.
